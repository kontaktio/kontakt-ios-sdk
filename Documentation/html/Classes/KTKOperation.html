Operation Methods

– execute

execute is the entry point of execution for all KTKOperation subclasses. If you subclass KTKOperation and wish to customize its execution, you would do so by overriding the execute method.

- (void)execute

Discussion

execute is the entry point of execution for all KTKOperation subclasses. If you subclass KTKOperation and wish to customize its execution, you would do so by overriding the execute method.

At some point, your KTKOperation subclass must call one of the finish methods defined below; this is how you indicate that your operation has finished its execution, and that operations dependent on yours can re-evaluate their readiness state.
